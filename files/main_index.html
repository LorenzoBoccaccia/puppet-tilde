tilde.town is a small unix server built for sharing

tilde.town is a clone of tilde.club. It is still new and has plenty of space for new users. tilde.town is completely free to use.

get an account

If you'd like a tilde.town account, just message me on twitter with your desired username and a link to a paste of your ssh pub key (preferably in ssh-rsa format).

you can also mail me at nks@lambdaphil.es. I tend to notice things on Twitter faster, though.

guidelines

this place is run exactly like tilde.club. You are free to refer to their FAQ/guidelines. We adhere to the same principles. In other words, no drama. No cruely. No trolls. I (~vilmibm) wield autocratric power and have no issues booting people who makes things unpleasant for others.

server

this runs on an ec2 micro using ubuntu 14.04. I am working on a puppet module that allows anyone to easily provision a tilde-style server.
